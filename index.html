I'm a third year Computer Science undergraduate at the University of British Columbia. I am a Teaching Assistant for Computer Science 110 (Systematic Program Design) and 210 (Software Construction with Java). And love it! (See fig.2, fig.3) I enjoy puzzles and thinking through proofs - which drew me to Computer Science.

Lately, I've been thinking about the *domain* of the puzzles I want to solve in my lifetime, which has led to start thinking about a second major in a Life Science. Pharmacology? Biochemistry? Proteomics? They all seem so ripe with complexity - and ready to be computationally reaped!

I'm a very proud former member of the Military Medicine Institute (Singapore). I did not think much of myself prior to my time there and I have my commanders to thank for uncovering a lot of the better parts of who I am today. (See fig.2)
