Simple Linear Regression with tensorflow.js




I drank too much coffee and watched this tutorial. Shout out to the people at google for making such awesome tools and then making resources for people like me to learn from!

Analysis

This was very simple. The data was generated from a simple y = x formula, then the y-values were given a random additive on [-2.3,2.3]. Then the model fit a 2D sample matrix over ~100 epochs. A prediction vector was extracted from the model along the original x-values and then rendered as a line via chart.js.

Errors were present, given the small sample size, there were sizable deviations from the y=x line.
